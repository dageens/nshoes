From contemporary looks inspired by the catwalk to shoes that put growing feet in safe hands, one name says it all: Nolans shoes. We design, innovate, manufacture and sell the best quality pairs of shoes every year. We sell cutting-edge 'fast fashion' and offer a wide variety of fashion-related content. Founded in 2000 by friends Philip and Jack. Nolans is small business, selling qualitative shoes in Ireland. While we continue to grow and innovate, we never lose sight of the values that have made us the business we are today – treating our customers, partners, suppliers and each other with honesty, integrity and respect.
